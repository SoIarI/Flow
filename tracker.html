📞 Call Script
Step 01 — Opening
The hook (first 10 sec)
"Hi, is this [name]? Great — I'm [your name] from Pulra, we run the POS at your restaurant. Do you have 2 minutes? I wanted to reach you before we open this to everyone."
Step 02 — Setup
Curiosity, not a pitch
"We've added online ordering that feeds straight into your existing POS — same screen your staff uses. No new device, no training. And since you're already a customer, you get first access and it's free right now."
Step 03 — Ask
Close for the meeting
"I'd love to pop by and show you — 15 minutes max. Would this week or next work? Happy to come to you."
Objection 01
We use [other app] already
They say: "We're on [delivery app], we're fine."
"This works alongside it — orders here go straight into your current system. It's a second channel you keep 100% of. Worth 15 min?"
Objection 02
Too busy
They say: "Not a good time."
"Can I come early morning or after lunch rush? Even 10 minutes is enough."
Objection 03
What's the catch?
They say: "Why free?"
"Launch phase — we want our best customers on first. Early partners get the best terms, locked in."
Objection 04
We don't do delivery
They say: "We don't handle delivery."
"You don't have to — logistics are handled. You just prepare the order like any other."
Step 04 — Close
Confirm the meeting
"Perfect — I'll see you [day] at [time]. I'll send a message to confirm. Looking forward to it."

→ Log the lead right after. Set a follow-up date.
